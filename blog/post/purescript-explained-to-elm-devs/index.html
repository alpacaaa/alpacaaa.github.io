Purescript for Elm developers.

Nov 12, 2017

I’ve been running Elm in production since March 2017.

Purescript always caught my eyes as a very interesting language and I’ve been learning it more and more over the past couple of months. I found that getting productive with Purescript is not as straightforward as it could be for people familiar with Elm.

So I put together a document highlighting the most significant differences between the two languages. You can find it here:

https://github.com/alpacaaa/elm-to-purescript-cheatsheet

I suggest you give Purescript a try.
